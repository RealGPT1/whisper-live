How it works

Voice activity detection

RMS energy threshold with a draggable marker to tune mic sensitivity.

WebGPU acceleration

Uses the GPU when available, falls back to WASM automatically.

Fully private

Audio never leaves your device. Model is cached after first download.

Keyboard shortcuts

Space to start/stop. R to force-transcribe the current buffer.
